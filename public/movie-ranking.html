The Best Movies According to Aaron

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Shindlers list

Ultimately, what makes "Schindler's List" such a great movie is its ability to tell a story that is both heart-wrenching and inspiring. The film is a testament to the power of human decency and the importance of standing up for what is right, even in the face of unimaginable evil. It is a movie that stays with you long after the credits have rolled

All Quiet on the Western Front

Ultimately, what makes "All Quiet on the Western Front" such a great movie is its ability to convey a sense of universal human experience. While the film is set during World War I and is based on a German novel, its themes of loss, disillusionment, and the toll of war are universal and speak to audiences of all nationalities and backgrounds.

City of God

What makes "City of God" such a great movie is its ability to humanize the people who live in the slums of Rio de Janeiro. The film shows that even in the midst of poverty and violence, there is still hope and humanity to be found.
